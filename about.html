Drishti app is a cross-platform mobile application that recognizes different Nepalese currency notes. It is mainly targeted for the use of blind and visually impaired individuals. This user-friendly app uses the flashlight, phone accessibility feature, and gestures to provide desired results. The Drishti app has been designed for two modes: quick mode and advanced mode. In the quick mode, the app can scan a note in real-time and give accurate results. While in the advanced mode, the scanned/used notes can be summed up to show the history of the transactions made in a day.
